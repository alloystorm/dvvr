## New Advanced Motion Smoothing

DanceXR 2026.2 introduces an advanced physics-based motion smoothing algorithm that not only enhances the fluidity of dance movements but also simulates realistic acceleration and damping effects, recreating natural motion dynamics. 

This new algorithm reduces latency compared to the previous version, providing a more responsive experience. It also offers customizable parameters, allowing users to fine-tune the smoothing effect to their preferences.

The smoothing featured is turned on by default for all motions, and you can choose to disable it or use the settings to fine tuen the smoothing parameters for different joints individually.

We've also improved audio timing synchronization to further improve responsiveness and eliminates occational stuttering caused by audio and motion desynchronization.

## Upgraded to Unity 6.3
Upgraded the underlying Unity engine to version 6.3, bringing performance enhancements, improved graphics capabilities, and access to the latest features and tools for a better overall experience.

In particular, Unity 6.3 comes with DLSS version 310.4.0.0, enhancing performance and visual quality on supported NVIDIA GPUs.

## Shader Updates
Improved skin texture and sweat effects with updated shaders, providing a more realistic appearance for characters.
