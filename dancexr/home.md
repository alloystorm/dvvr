# Animate Any Model, Anywhere

**DanceXR** is a powerful character model viewer and motion player for PMX/MMD and XNALara/XPS models — available on PC, Mac, Android, and Quest VR. Load your favorite models, play dance motions, and create stunning scenes with no manual bone tweaking required.

[Try for Free](#download) | [Buy on Steam](#steam) | [Patreon](#patreon)

---

## Just Load and Play

Most tools require you to manually fix bones, adjust poses, and tweak settings for every model-motion combination. DanceXR handles it automatically.

- **Universal motion compatibility** — VMD and BVH motions play correctly on any model, whether it was built for T-pose or A-pose
- **On-the-fly bone fixes** — Common bone structure issues are corrected automatically, so you spend time enjoying content, not debugging it
- **Supports both PMX and XPS** — Full physics, material settings, and pose support for both model formats

---

## Alive by Default

Characters in DanceXR don't just stand still. Without any setup, they breathe, blink, and make eye contact. Load a model and it already feels present.

- Procedural breathing and blinking
- Natural eye contact that follows you
- Idle motions and catwalk animations to keep scenes lively
- **Auto Dance** — characters generate dance moves in real time, driven by music beats and volume

---

## Physics That Move

DanceXR includes a full simulation stack running in real time:

- **Cloth simulation** — Stable, parallel-threaded cloth physics with tearing, dragging, and mesh-to-cloth conversion
- **Hair, skirt, and soft body physics** — Per-model configuration for XPS and PMX models
- **Particle dynamics** — Flowing hair, fabric, and secondary motion that reacts to animation

---

## Talk to Your Characters

With AI-powered voice chat, your characters respond to you in real time:

- Connect to **OpenAI**, a local LLM (LM Studio, Ollama, Ooba), or a remote GPU service
- **Built-in TTS** with 900+ voices — each character can have their own
- **Automatic lipsync** — faces animate as they speak
- **Voice recognition** — talk to them, not just type
- Customize personality, persona, and scenario through simple templates
