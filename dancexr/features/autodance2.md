# Auto Dance 2

<!-- TODO: confirm settings. Drafted from procedural-motion family. -->

Auto Dance 2 is the **second-generation** procedural dance generator. It improves on the original [Auto Dance](autodance) with a larger motion pool, better variation between consecutive moves, and a cleaner sync to the music's beats and volume.

Auto Dance 2 is itself superseded by [Auto Dance 3](autodance3), which uses the modern motion control system shared with [Sex Motion 3](sex_motion_3) and integrates with the realtime audio analyzer.

---

## What changed from Auto Dance 1

- Larger built-in pool of dance segments.
- Better blending between consecutive moves so the result reads less like a slideshow.
- Improved response to music volume — higher-energy passages get bigger moves.

## When to choose Auto Dance 2

Use Auto Dance 2 when you want a procedural dance that is:

- More varied than the original Auto Dance.
- Simpler than the highly configurable Auto Dance 3 — fewer knobs, faster to set up.

For new projects, prefer [Auto Dance 3](autodance3) unless you specifically want Auto Dance 2's behavior.
