Cowgirl Sex Motion is the **first** procedural NSFW motion in DanceXR — fixed to a cowgirl position, generating thrust and bounce timing automatically without a VMD source.

## What it does

- Generates the rhythmic motion for a cowgirl-position pair: female on top, male on bottom.
- Bounces and thrust timing tied to either music BPM or manual speed.

For broader pose support, contact-frame alignment, arousal / orgasm simulation, and easier tuning, use [Sex Motion 3](sex_motion_3) instead.
