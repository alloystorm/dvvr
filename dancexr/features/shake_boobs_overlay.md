# Shake Boobs Overlay

Adds rhythmic breast shake motion driven by the music beat.
**Intensity** sets the amplitude; **Speed** scales the beat
frequency (power-of-2). **Motion Pattern** blends between
horizontal-only (−1), vertical-only (1), or combined (0)
shake direction.

**Time Pattern** selects the rhythm: *Constant* shakes every
beat; *Pulse* alternates on/off every other beat; *Gradual*
follows a predefined 16-beat pattern that builds intensity
over phrases. **Abs Mode** bounces back at the neutral
position for a more exaggerated effect.
