# Recently Modified Settings

A quick-access list of the per-actor settings you have changed most recently. Lets you re-open the same dialog without navigating back through the actor menu hierarchy.

Available from the actor menu — see [Actor menu & tools](/dancexr/features/actor_tools#actor-menu).

---

## Why it exists

The actor menu has many sections — materials, physics, dressing, motion, behavior. When you are iterating on a single feature (tweaking hair physics, comparing two skin material setups), it is faster to jump straight back to the most recently used dialog than to drill down again.

Recently Modified Settings gives you that shortcut. The list updates automatically as you change settings; the most recent change goes to the top.

---

## Using it

<!-- TODO: confirm exact UI behavior. Below is what is implied by the actor_tools menu reference. -->

1. Open the actor menu (click the actor's selection disc).
2. Click **Recently Modified** near the top of the menu.
3. The list shows the most recent settings you adjusted on this actor, with the most recent on top.
4. Click an entry to jump to that dialog.

The list is per-actor and resets when you reload the model or the scene.
