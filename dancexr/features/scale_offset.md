# Scale & Offset

Adjusts the actor's overall size, position, and rotation in
the scene.

**Model Scale** uses a power-of-2 exponential scale so small
changes produce fine adjustments. **Uniform Height** scales
all actors to a consistent average human height regardless of
their original model proportions — disable this when you want
to preserve the model's intended size. **Ground Offset** moves
the actor up or down relative to the floor.

**Rotation** turns the actor around the Y axis. **Offset X**
and **Offset Z** shift position horizontally. **Snapping**
sets the increment size when dragging the actor in the scene —
zero disables snapping entirely.

Presets include *Uniform Life Size*, *Miniature*, *Giant*,
and *Original*.
