# Lips Materials

<!-- TODO: confirm exact settings exposed in the lips submenu. Drafted from sister material pages. -->

Controls properties of the materials categorized as **lips**. Lips materials are slightly more reflective and translucent than other skin areas, which is what gives lips their characteristic wet sheen.

Lips materials gained their own preset list in **2024.1**.

---

## What this category controls

- Base color tint (lipstick-like recoloring without editing the texture).
- Smoothness / roughness — controls the wet sheen.

## Categorization

DanceXR auto-assigns materials whose names match common lip keywords to this category. If a model misclassifies its lips, manually move materials into the lips category from the [Material Settings](material_settings) list.
