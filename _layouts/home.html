Universal compatibility

Bring any character to life.

DanceXR loads virtually any MMD (PMX) or XNALara/XPS model and plays VMD or BVH motion files — without manual bone adjustments. The built-in pose converter handles T-pose and A-pose models automatically, fixing common rig issues on the fly.
